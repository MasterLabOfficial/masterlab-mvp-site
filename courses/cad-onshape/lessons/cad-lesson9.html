CAD Lesson 9 – Drawings & Dimensioning Standards

Drawings communicate how a part should be manufactured. They show views, dimensions, and notes in a standardized way.

  • Front, Top, Right views (orthographic).
  • Isometric view for 3D clarity.
  • Dimension to important features, not random edges.
  • Avoid duplicating dimensions.
  • Keep dimensions outside the part when possible.
  • Notes for material, finish, or special instructions.
  • Hole callouts for drilled or tapped holes.
