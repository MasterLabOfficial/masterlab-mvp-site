CAD Lesson 4 – Core 3D Features: Extrude, Revolve, Cut

Once sketches are ready, you turn them into 3D geometry using features. The most important ones in Onshape are Extrude, Revolve, and various Cut options.

  • Turns a 2D sketch into a 3D solid or cut.
  • Key options: Blind, Symmetric, Up to Next, Through All.
  • Can add material (solid) or remove material (cut).
  • Rotates a sketch profile around an axis.
  • Used for round parts: shafts, knobs, wheels, bottles.
  • Requires an axis of revolution (often a centerline).
  • Extrude Remove: cuts material using a sketch.
  • Revolve Remove: cuts by revolving a profile.
  • Use "Through All" to guarantee cuts fully pass through.
