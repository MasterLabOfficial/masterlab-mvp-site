CAD Lesson 5 – Fillets, Chamfers, Shell & Patterns

Detail features refine the shape of your model. They are usually added after the main form is established.

  • Round edges or corners.
  • Used to reduce stress, improve appearance, or match real parts.
  • Beveled edges instead of sharp corners.
  • Common on machined parts and edges that need clearance.
  • Hollows out a solid body to a specified wall thickness.
  • Useful for enclosures, cases, and hollow parts.
  • Linear Pattern: Repeats features in a straight line.
  • Circular Pattern: Repeats features around an axis.
  • Use patterns instead of manually recreating repeated features.
